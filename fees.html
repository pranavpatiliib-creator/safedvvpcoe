Full process

Step-by-step online payment flow

Follow these steps in order to avoid errors during registration, login, payment, and receipt download.

01

Collect your details

Keep your full name, mobile number, email address, PRN, course, branch, year, and Aadhaar details ready before starting.

02

Register as a new user

If you are using the portal for the first time, open the registration page and enter all personal and academic details correctly.

03

Create secure login details

Choose a password you can remember. Recheck your email and mobile number because they may be used for communication or recovery.

04

Login to your account

After registration, use the login page to enter your email and password. Returning students can start directly from this step.

05

Review fee details

Check the payable amount, fee category, and student information carefully. Confirm that all visible details match your admission records.

06

Complete online payment

Use the available online mode to submit payment. Do not refresh the page or close the browser window while the transaction is processing.

07

Check payment status

Once the transaction is completed, verify whether the status shows success, pending, or failed. If it is pending, wait and recheck before retrying.

08

Download and save the receipt

After successful payment, download the receipt and keep a copy for future reference, document verification, and administrative use.
