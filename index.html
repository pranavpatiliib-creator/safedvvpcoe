Department ethos

Our Foundation

Guiding principles that shape how we teach, mentor, and prepare first-year engineers.

V Aim

Vision

To create a strong academic foundation for first year engineering students by fostering innovation, critical thinking, and technical excellence. We aim to nurture future engineers who are capable of solving real-world problems with creativity and responsibility.

M Action

Mission

To provide high-quality education through a balanced approach of theory and practical learning. We encourage students to participate in technical activities, workshops, and collaborative projects that enhance their skills, confidence, and teamwork abilities.

D Outcome

Development

The department focuses on overall development of students by promoting academic growth, communication skills, leadership qualities, and participation in extracurricular activities. Special emphasis is given to innovation, problem-solving, and industry readiness.
