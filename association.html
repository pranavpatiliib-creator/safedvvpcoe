Student Association

The First Year Engineering Student Association is dedicated to building leadership, creativity, and technical excellence among students. It plays a vital role in shaping students' personalities through various academic and extracurricular activities.

Chairman of Student Association

Prof. (Dr.) S. A. Markad

Associate Professor and Head, First Year Engineering

As Chairman of the Student Association, Prof. (Dr.) S. A. Markad guides the department's student-led initiatives with a strong focus on discipline, innovation, and all-round development. His leadership encourages students to participate confidently in technical, cultural, and social activities while building the values of teamwork, responsibility, and academic excellence.

About the Association

The association acts as a platform for students to collaborate, innovate, and grow beyond academics. It encourages participation in technical events, cultural programs, and sports activities to ensure holistic development of every student.

Technical Events

Workshops, coding sessions, and project exhibitions.

Cultural Programs

Freshers, competitions, and celebrations.

Sports Activities

Indoor and outdoor sports competitions.

Skill Development

Communication, leadership, and personality growth.
